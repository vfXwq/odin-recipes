Lasagna Recipe

How to Make Lasagna

Making lasagna can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait. You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below, but let's go over the basics:

Lasagna Ingredients

Here are the ingredient you're gonna need to cook a delicious lasagna!

  • Meat: sweet italian sausage and ground beef.
  • Onion and garlic: these add tons of flavor to the lasagna!
  • Tomato products: you will need crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, and tomato paste.
  • Sugar: white sugger enchaces the flavor of the sause.
  • Spices and seasoning: you will need fresh parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and black pepper.
  • Lasagna noodles: either store-bought or homemade lasagna noodles.
  • Cheeses: you will need parmesan, mozarella, and ricotta cheese.
  • Egg: egg helps bind the lasagna.

Quick Overview

Here are some quick instructions on how to make a delicious lasagna.

  1. Make the meet sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the riccotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna accordingly to instructions below.
  5. Cover with foil and bake it at 375 degrees farenheit for 50 minutes.
  6. Let the lasagna rest for 15 minutes before cutting it and serving.

Layering instructions

Here's the order for layering your lasagna.

  1. Meat sauce
  2. Noodles
  3. Ricotta mixture
  4. Mozzarella slices
  5. Meat sauce
  6. Parmesan cheese
  7. Repeat the layers and top with the remaining parmesan in the end.
